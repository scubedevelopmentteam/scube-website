✦ Features

Everything you need to fill
your CRM from LinkedIn

From a single profile to bulk search results — LinkedBridge handles every step of the LinkedIn-to-CRM workflow.

🎯
Bulk Queue with Lead Scoring
Add profiles to a drag-and-drop queue as you browse. Each profile is automatically scored 0–100 based on title, industry, and data completeness. Batch-save the entire queue to Salesforce with one click.
  • Configurable keyword rules (VP/Director = high, Intern = low)
  • Notes and tags per item (ICP, warm, priority, met in person)
  • Bulk email enrichment across all queued profiles
  • Export queue to CSV before pushing to CRM
✨
3-Provider Enrichment
Find verified emails and phone numbers via Hunter.io, Apollo.io, or Clearbit. Accept or discard each enriched field individually before saving.
🔍
Smart Duplicate Detection
Before saving, the extension queries your CRM for matching email or LinkedIn URL. Shows a side-by-side comparison so you can update the existing record instead.
📊
Profile Completeness Scoring
Visual badge and progress ring show data quality before you save — so you know exactly what's missing.
🚀
Bulk Search & Post Scraping
On LinkedIn search results pages, scan all visible results and add them to your queue in one click. On posts and events, scrape everyone who reacted or RSVP'd — warm leads who already engaged with your content.
  • Search results: scan visible profiles → add all to queue
  • Post reactions: capture warm prospects who engaged
  • Event attendees: RSVP list → bulk queue → batch save
🏢
Company Page → Account
Extract full company data including tech stack, funding, employees, specialties, and recent posts. Save or update a Salesforce Account instantly.
🗂️
Custom Field Mapping
Map any LinkedIn field to any CRM field. Set character limits, enable/disable fields, and save different mapping profiles per CRM.
📎
Resume / CV Attachment
Attach a candidate's CV/resume directly to the Salesforce Lead record at save time — uploaded as a ContentVersion file.
